## üö® COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

### Mistake 1: FOMO Trading
**Example:** Stock already up 40%, you chase because "it's going higher"  
**Rule:** Wait for pullback. If it doesn't pull back, move on.

### Mistake 2: Stale Intel Trading
**Example:** DNN - "hearing any day" but hearing already happened  
**Rule:** Timestamp everything. Verify it's current.

### Mistake 3: Panic Selling
**Example:** DNN - sold on red, thesis wasn't broken  
**Rule:** Pre-set stop loss. If hit, exit. Otherwise, hold if thesis intact.

### Mistake 4: No-Thesis Speculation
**Example:** NTLA - bought without clear reason  
**Rule:** If you can't explain WHY in one sentence, don't trade it.

### Mistake 5: Ignoring Convergence
**Example:** Trading 30/100 convergence because "I like the stock"  
**Rule:** Convergence <50 = system says NO. Listen to the system.
